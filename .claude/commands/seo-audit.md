Audit the SEO of a blog post or page.

File to audit: $ARGUMENTS

Check and report on:
1. **Title tag** — is it under 60 chars, does it include primary keyword?
2. **Meta description** — is it 150-160 chars, compelling, includes keyword?
3. **H1/H2/H3 structure** — proper hierarchy, keywords in headings?
4. **Keyword density** — is the primary keyword used naturally 3-5 times?
5. **Internal links** — links to other blog posts and valooptimise.com?
6. **Image alt tags** — do all images have descriptive alt text?
7. **URL/filename** — is it keyword-rich and hyphenated?
8. **Content length** — is it 1500+ words for competitive ranking?
9. **FAQ section** — present for featured snippet potential?
10. **Mobile readability** — short paragraphs, scannable subheadings?

Output a score out of 10 and specific fixes for each issue found.
